TwistiCity: Our Story

Do you say Twisticity, or do you say TwistiCity?  YES!


TwistiCity began when my then 13-year-old aspiring-artist son asked how he could make money for art supplies by selling his art.  I knew I could draw a small crowd with balloons so he could offer to draw people's requests for tips.  One Chicago street-performer's license later, and we were in business- twisting and drawing for tips.  As one thing often leads to another, I began getting requests to do parties and events. and soon... TwistiCity was born! Since joining forces with my super-talented daughter Anna, our balloon repertoire has grown dramatically as well as the types of places we perform...Birthday Parties, Block Parties, Farmer's Markets, Park District events, Street Festivals, Champagne Brunches (no joke!) . Anna, who is a natural with kids, has really blossomed as a face painter.  Add TwistiCity Music ,and we can bring the power of music, puppets, and balloons to your party or event. Did I mention the Giant Bubbles?! We would love to make your next event super-amazing in our special way!
